EvoChess

A new world of chess awaits! Are you ready?!

Chess got a whole lot interesting. As time goes on everything adapts and evolves so why not this amazing game. We all know how intense chess battles are, now with a twist how will all you chess grandmasters handle this?

What's new :

Imagine being a pawn fighting for your kingdom, with despair smacking against your face in the midst of battle. No where to go but foward towards victory. With your heart pumping and adrenaline rushing through your body. You slay your enemies one by one. You look down at your hands and feel like hero in your own story. Now is your chance to be something more!!!

  • Pawns can now evolve!
  • Every chess piece is assigned a certain number of points
    1. Queens are worth the most with 7 points
    2. Rooks are worth 5 points
    3. Bishops and knights are worth 3 points
    4. Pawns are worth 1 point
  • Each time a pawn eliminates one of these pieces it evolves
